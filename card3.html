Welcome to Temple Coffee St51

Our Story

Temple Coffee St51 is a specialty coffee shop located in the heart of Phnom Penh. Our goal is to bring the best coffee experience to our customers, offering a selection of carefully sourced beans, expertly brewed coffee, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're looking to relax or get some work done, Temple Coffee provides the perfect environment for every occasion.

Our Coffee

At Temple Coffee St51, we offer a variety of high-quality coffee blends and single-origin beans. Our beans are sourced from renowned coffee-growing regions and roasted to perfection to ensure the freshest and most flavorful cups of coffee. Our baristas are passionate about coffee and always ready to create the perfect brew for you.

Customer Favorites

  • Espresso: A rich, bold shot of coffee for true coffee lovers.
  • Cappuccino: A smooth blend of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a dusting of cocoa powder.
  • Flat White: A perfect balance of espresso and velvety steamed milk.

Visit Us

Temple Coffee St51 is located in a vibrant part of Phnom Penh, making it the ideal place for a morning coffee, a break during work, or a place to meet with friends. Our café is designed with a cozy and modern interior, providing a relaxing atmosphere for all our guests.
