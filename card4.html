Welcome to NOIR COFFEE Yukunthor

Our Story

NOIR Coffee Yukunthor is more than just a café; it’s a place where you can savor the finest coffee in the heart of Phnom Penh. Our coffee shop is designed to offer a cozy atmosphere, perfect for those seeking great coffee and a place to unwind or work. We focus on quality, sustainability, and creating a memorable experience for our customers.

Our Coffee

We serve a variety of premium coffee blends, each crafted with care to bring out the best flavors. Our beans are ethically sourced and roasted to perfection, ensuring every cup of coffee is fresh, flavorful, and satisfying. From bold espressos to creamy lattes, there’s something for everyone.

Customer Favorites

  • Signature Espresso: A robust, full-bodied espresso shot perfect for purists.
  • Caramel Latte: A smooth espresso with steamed milk and a touch of caramel sweetness.
  • Iced Coffee: A refreshing choice for hot days, served cold with a rich flavor.

Visit Us

We are located at the Yukunthor area in Phnom Penh. Our café is a relaxing space for those who enjoy a great cup of coffee and a comfortable ambiance. Whether you’re here to study, work, or enjoy a coffee with friends, NOIR Coffee Yukunthor is the place to be.
