Welcome to PLENG CHAN (BKK)

Our Story

PLENG CHAN (BKK) is a hidden gem in Phnom Penh, offering a warm and inviting space for coffee lovers. With a focus on quality brews and a relaxing atmosphere, we strive to create the perfect spot for work, study, or simply unwinding with a great cup of coffee.

Our Coffee

We serve a variety of freshly brewed coffees, from rich espressos to smooth cold brews. Every cup is crafted with care, using high-quality beans to ensure a satisfying experience.

Customer Favorites

  • Pleng Chan Signature Latte: A creamy blend with hints of caramel and vanilla.
  • Traditional Khmer Coffee: A strong and aromatic brew, perfect for those who love bold flavors.
  • Refreshing Iced Americano: A crisp and energizing drink, great for hot days.
